This release focused on stability improvements and smoothing out core features.

  • AddedTips! Tips on how the gamemode works and how to play it now show up on the screens of newcomers

  • AddedCowboy slur to the in-game chat. YEEHAW
  • AddedNew sound effect when a player becomes downed
